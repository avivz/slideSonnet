# Adjacency

Two vertices are **adjacent** if connected by an edge.

<!-- say: Two vertices are adjacent if there is an edge between them. The set of all neighbors of a vertex v is called its neighborhood. -->

---

# Degree

The **degree** of a vertex is the number of edges incident to it.

<!-- say: The degree of a vertex is the number of edges connected to it. A vertex with degree zero is called an isolated vertex. -->
